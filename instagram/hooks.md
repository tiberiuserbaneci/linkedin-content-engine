# Instagram Hooks — 10 Hooks for Ultron

Two versions per hook:
- SPOKEN: for Reels (first 3 seconds of audio — must stop the scroll before the skip)
- VISUAL: for Carousel Slide 1 (text on screen, under 8 words)

---

## Hook 1 — Dispatch vs Chat

**SPOKEN:** "You're talking to AI. I'm giving mine orders."

**VISUAL:** "Prompting AI vs deploying it."

---

## Hook 2 — Cross-Agent Chain

**SPOKEN:** "My AI found the leads, wrote the emails, and logged every step. I reviewed. Then I hit send."

**VISUAL:** "AI that finds leads and writes emails."

---

## Hook 3 — Zero-Task Discipline

**SPOKEN:** "My AI agents do nothing without a task. On purpose."

**VISUAL:** "AI that refuses to freelance."

---

## Hook 4 — External Data

**SPOKEN:** "Claude knows what happened in 2024. Ultron searched the web 3 minutes ago."

**VISUAL:** "Training data vs live search."

---

## Hook 5 — Human Gate

**SPOKEN:** "My AI drafts everything. Sends nothing. Until I say go."

**VISUAL:** "AI that asks before it sends."

---

## Hook 6 — Memory Compounds

**SPOKEN:** "Every mistake my AI makes gets saved as a lesson. It reads those lessons every time it starts."

**VISUAL:** "An AI that learns from its own mistakes."

---

## Hook 7 — ICP Scoring

**SPOKEN:** "Your cold email problem isn't the copy. You're emailing people who were never going to reply."

**VISUAL:** "Stop sending cold emails to the wrong list."

---

## Hook 8 — Audit Trail

**SPOKEN:** "I can show you every action my AI took at 3am, why it took it, and what it found."

**VISUAL:** "Full audit trail. Every AI action. Every source."

---

## Hook 9 — Claude as Engine

**SPOKEN:** "Claude is the brain. Ultron is the OS that makes it work on your actual business."

**VISUAL:** "Claude + Ultron. One system. Real output."

---

## Hook 10 — Model Routing

**SPOKEN:** "Different AI models do different things well. Routing them correctly is the skill most founders skip."

**VISUAL:** "Claude for reasoning. GPT for speed. Route them right."

---

## Usage Notes
- Hooks 5, 7, and 9 test best with cold audiences (clear contrast, no prior context needed).
- Hook 2 and Hook 8 work best paired with a dashboard screenshot or activity feed capture.
- For Reels: deliver spoken hooks at normal pace — no dramatic pause. Conversational, not scripted.
- For Carousel: visual hook text should be the only element on Slide 1. No subtitle. No logo on hook slide.
