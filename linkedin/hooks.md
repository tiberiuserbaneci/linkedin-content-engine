# LinkedIn Hooks — 10 Scroll-Stoppers for Ultron

Each hook is a single sentence. No context. No setup. Pattern interrupt first.
Angle mapping noted after each hook.

---

## Hook 1 — Dispatch vs Chat (Angle 1)
> You're not prompting AI. You're watching it work while you sleep.

---

## Hook 2 — Zero-Task Discipline (Angle 2)
> My AI agents do nothing without an assigned task. That's a feature, not a bug.

---

## Hook 3 — External Data (Angle 3)
> Claude trained on data from 2024. Ultron's research module searched the web 3 minutes ago.

---

## Hook 4 — Cross-Agent Chaining (Angle 6)
> The research module found the leads. The sales engine wrote the emails. I read the replies.

---

## Hook 5 — ICP Scoring Before Outreach (Angle 8)
> Your cold email problem isn't the copy. It's that you're emailing the wrong people.

---

## Hook 6 — Memory Compounds (Angle 4)
> Every mistake Ultron makes gets stored as a lesson. It reads those lessons before the next run.

---

## Hook 7 — Human Gate (Angle 5)
> Autonomous AI that sends emails without asking you first is not a product. It's a liability.

---

## Hook 8 — Audit Trail as Product (Angle 7)
> I can tell you exactly what my AI did at 3:17am, why it did it, and what it found.

---

## Hook 9 — Claude as the Brain
> Claude is the reasoning engine. Ultron is the OS that puts it to work on your actual business.

---

## Hook 10 — Model Routing as Advantage
> GPT-4o for speed. Claude for reasoning. Gemini for context windows. Routing them right is the skill nobody talks about.

---

## Usage Notes
- Use one hook per post. Never stack two in the same caption.
- Pair with a visual that creates contrast (dashboard screenshot, blank screen, or plain text card).
- Test Hook 5 and Hook 9 first — highest VIRALS Integration score for the ICP.
- Hook 3 works best when you show a real timestamp from Ultron's activity log.
