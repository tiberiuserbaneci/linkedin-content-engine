# LinkedIn Carousel Scripts — 3 Carousels for Ultron

Format: Slide 1 = Hook (<8 words visual text). Slides 2-5 = Key points (15 words max per slide).
Slide 6 = CTA. Optional Slide 7 = Payoff/source.

Visual notes included. VIRALS score at the end of each carousel.

---

## Carousel 2 — "VIRALS Score: The Framework Ultron Uses Before Publishing Anything"
**Angle:** Give away the internal framework as educational value. Builds trust + authority.
**Target:** Content creators, founders who post on LinkedIn, GTM leads who run content programs.

---

**Slide 1 — Hook**
Text: "Ultron won't publish content that scores below 7."
Visual: A scoring card with 6 rows (V / I / R / A / L / S) and a red warning at "6/12."

---

**Slide 2**
Text: "V — Values. Does this make the reader look smart or give them something useful?"
Visual: Two examples — generic AI post (crossed out) vs specific framework post (checked).

---

**Slide 3**
Text: "I — Integration. Does this hook into their daily work, role, or current moment?"
Visual: Text: "A post about 'AI trends' = low I. A post about 'Claude prompt routing in prod' = high I."

---

**Slide 4**
Text: "R — Resonates. Does it trigger a real emotion? Relief, curiosity, anger, awe."
Visual: Four emotional state icons with example triggers beneath each.

---

**Slide 5**
Text: "A — Action. Does it invite participation? Comment, share, try, vote, save."
Visual: CTA examples — "comment YES if this happened to you" / "save this for your next sprint."

---

**Slide 6**
Text: "L — Leverage. Does it hand them a practical tool? Script, checklist, template, formula."
Visual: "Posts that teach get saves. Posts that give templates get shares."

---

**Slide 7 — CTA**
Text: "S — Stories. Every post that compounds has a moment. A before. An after. A tension."
Sub-text: "Score your next post before you hit publish. Below 7 means rewrite."
Visual: Full VIRALS scorecard template. Clean grid. Ultron logo bottom right.

---

**VIRALS Score: 10/12** — V:2 I:2 R:1 A:2 L:2 S:1

## Carousel 3 — "ICP Scoring 1-10: How the Research Module Qualifies Leads"
**Angle:** Sales education rooted in Ultron's real internal process. Practical, immediately applicable.
**Target:** Founders doing outreach, GTM leads, sales-led or sales-assisted startups.

---

**Slide 1 — Hook**
Text: "Most cold emails fail before the copy is written."
Visual: An inbox with 50 sent emails, 0 replies. Red badge.

---

**Slide 2**
Text: "The problem is not the email. It's that 80% of the list shouldn't receive one."
Visual: A lead list — 20 highlighted in green (ICP fit), 80 in gray (not qualified).

---

**Slide 3**
Text: "Ultron's research module scores every lead 1-10 before the sales engine touches the list."
Visual: A lead card with fields — company, size, tech stack, recent activity, ICP score: 8.5/10.

---

**Slide 4**
Text: "Score 8-10: immediate outreach task auto-created for the sales engine."
Visual: Lead card with score 9 → arrow → task card "Write cold email to [Name] at [Company]."

---

**Slide 5**
Text: "Score 5-7: enters nurture. Score 1-4: archived. No manual sorting. No spreadsheet."
Visual: Three columns — Hot (green) / Warm (yellow) / Cold (gray). Leads auto-routing into each.

---

**Slide 6**
Text: "What gets scored: company size, tech stack, recent news, job postings, ICP role match."
Visual: Six scoring criteria shown as a checklist. Each with a 0-2 point weight.

---

**Slide 7 — CTA**
Text: "Your cold email isn't broken. Your list is."
Sub-text: "Follow for how to build a research-first outreach system."
Visual: Ultron logo. Clean dark background.
