## 🎯 VISUAL HIERARCHY

### Type Scale
- **Overline:** 11px, uppercase, BookCloth accent (draws eye to date/context)
- **Headline:** 68px, bold, slate-light (commands attention, 3 lines max)
- **Subheading:** 17px, cloud-medium (tells the story)
- **Section labels:** 10px, uppercase, cloud-dark (scannable dividers)
- **Body:** 13–18px, varying by zone (readable, not dense)
- **Verbs/chips:** 12–12.5px, medium weight (compact, scannable)
- **Quote:** 17px, italic, ivory-dark (feels authoritative)

### Color Strategy
- **Background:** Slate Dark (#191919) — premium, stops scroll
- **Primary text:** Ivory Dark/Light (high contrast, accessible)
- **Accent:** BookCloth (#CC785C) — ONE warm color used sparingly for emphasis
- **Secondary:** Cloud grays (cloud-dark, cloud-medium, cloud-light) for muted context
- **Focus:** Not used for this asset (interactive connectors don't need blue CTAs here)
- **Borders:** 1px slate-light (subtle, premium, never jarring)

### Spacing Rules (8px grid)
- **Padding:** 64px top, 80px left/right, 56px bottom (generous)
- **Gaps:** 28–48px between zones (breathing room)
- **Internal:** 12–16px within cards (standard Anthropic spacing)
- **Text margins:** 14–24px below headings (hierarchy clarity)

### Contrast & Accessibility
- **Background to text:** 9:1+ (WCAG AAA compliant)
- **Muted text:** Cloud-medium on slate-dark still meets 7:1
- **Accent to background:** BookCloth on slate = 4.7:1 (readable, not primary text)
- **All text:** Anti-aliased, font-smoothing enabled

## 🎬 What Makes This Work

### 1. **Premium Feel**
- Dark, restrained color palette (Anthropic-native)

- Clean typography hierarchy with breathing room
- No gradients, no glows, no clichés

### 2. **Stop-Scroll Factor**
- Headline directly challenges founder assumptions ("Most teams still think...")
- 68px font size = impossible to miss

- Reframes from "new feature" → "operating model shift"

### 5. **Highly Saveable**
- "Why This Matters" section (Zone 6) = 4 concrete, quotable bullets
- "Founders Wrong" section = provocative enough to argue about
- Bottom quote = signature line, easy to cite
- Entire asset = no filler, all signal

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

This asset follows the **Anthropic Design Skill** established earlier:

✅ **Restraint** — 13 colors max, only BookCloth as accent  
✅ **Warmth** — Terracotta BookCloth, not cold blues  
✅ **Material thinking** — Named colors, not generic "primary"  
✅ **Accessibility** — WCAG AAA contrast, focus rings not needed  
✅ **Generosity** — 8px grid, breathing room, never cramped  
✅ **Simplicity** — No gradients, no effects, pure substance  

The result: **Premium enough to stop scroll. Strategic enough to share. Credible enough to believe.**
