# TikTok Hooks — 10 Spoken 3-Second Openers for Ultron

Pure spoken delivery. No intro. No "hey guys." No setup.
The hook IS the first sentence. Say it and mean it.

---

## Hook 1 — Dispatch vs Chat
"You're prompting AI. I'm dispatching it."

---

## Hook 2 — Cross-Agent Chain
"My AI found the leads, wrote the emails, and logged every step. I just approved them."

---

## Hook 3 — External Data
"Claude trained on data from 2024. Mine searched the web 4 minutes ago."

---

## Hook 4 — Zero-Task Discipline
"My AI agents do nothing without an assigned task. That's the whole point."

---

## Hook 5 — Human Gate
"My AI drafts everything. Sends nothing. Until I say go."

---

## Hook 6 — Memory Compounds
"Every mistake my AI makes gets stored. It reads those mistakes before the next run starts."

---

## Hook 7 — Audit Trail
"I can show you every action my AI took at 3am, the exact source it used, and why."

---

## Hook 8 — ICP Scoring
"Stop sending cold emails to the wrong people. Your AI can score the list before you write a word."

---

## Hook 9 — Model Routing
"You're using one AI model for everything. That's the bottleneck nobody talks about."

---

## Hook 10 — Claude as the Brain
"Claude is the reasoning engine. The question is whether you're giving it a system or just a prompt."

---

## Delivery Notes
- Say each hook like you're calling someone out. Not aggressive — certain.
- No pause before the hook. Hit record and go.
- Hooks 1, 5, and 9 work best as cold opens with no visual context.
- Hook 7 and Hook 2 pair well with a dashboard screen share behind you.
- Hook 3 is a factual statement — say it flat, let the contrast land on its own.
