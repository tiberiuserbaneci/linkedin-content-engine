## Post Structure

```
HOOK (2 lines exactly)
↓
Creates curiosity, stops scroll

BODY (14+ paragraphs, 10-19 words each)
↓
Active voice, one idea per paragraph, real examples

END (Repost or save prompt)
↓
Creates engagement signal

CTA (First comment ONLY)
↓
Format: "Ultron [benefit] → [URL]"
Never in post body
```
